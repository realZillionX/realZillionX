## 🧭 &nbsp; About

I'm an undergraduate at **Fudan University**, doing research at the **FDU NLP Lab** and the **OpenMOSS Team** under the supervision of **Prof. Xipeng Qiu**. My work sits at the intersection of **multimodal reasoning** and **agentic reinforcement learning** — building systems that look, think, act, and improve.

## 🔬 &nbsp; Research Interests

<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">
