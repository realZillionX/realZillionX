## 🧭 &nbsp; About

I'm an undergraduate at **Fudan University**, doing research at the **FDU NLP Lab** and the **OpenMOSS Team** under the supervision of **Prof. Xipeng Qiu**.

My work sits at the intersection of **multimodal reasoning** and **agentic reinforcement learning** — building systems that look, think, act, and improve.

---

## 🔬 &nbsp; Research Interests

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🧠 &nbsp; Multimodal Reasoning

Teaching models to reason jointly across vision, language, and structure — and exploring what makes that reasoning robust beyond surface pattern-matching.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🤖 &nbsp; Agentic RL
